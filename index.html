The above dog graph was made by creating a vector drawing a dog with a single line, using the SVG format (which is just XML).
First, before you continue you must flip the image of your dog upside down, otherwise the dog will be upside down in the graph!.
Second convert the SVG to polygon format from the default of paths to clean up the coords.
Third, grab the xy coords from the conversion and format them into seperate array groups (apart of one giant array). Lastly, insert the arrays into the javaScript for generating a Google Line Graph and you will have your Dog Graph!
My favorite part about dog graph is google has to re-generate the whole thing each time, there is 75,382 characters for the coords of this dog graph! Thats a lot of dog graph!
